IPA Research Protocols

Every research project at IPA is required to follow research protocols, or “Minimum Must Dos” to ensure that IPA produces high-quality research.

IPA’s Research Protocols are organized into three main categories. Click on each protocol below to learn more.

Data Management System - DMS
  • IPA Data Management System (IPA-DMS)

The IPA Data Management System (IPA-DMS) is a fundamental tool that standardizes data quality practices across IPA projects. It provides a structured framework for data cleaning, documentation, and quality control throughout the research lifecycle.
